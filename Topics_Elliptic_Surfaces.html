Syllabus

Course description

The study of elliptic fibrations f : X → C of an elliptic surface X over a curve C (i.e. family of elliptic curves over a global field) lies at the heart of surface classification.
John Tate (1925-2019) gave us a wealth of essential mathematical ideas and constructions in algebraic number theory and arithmetic geometry.
In this class, I will unpack the classical Tate's algorithm by explicitly working with polynomials and drawing the procedure by the help of the theory of moduli stacks.
