Syllabus

Course description

All polynomials are equal but some polynomials are more equal.

This statement is a play on words and is a reference to George Orwell's novel "Animal Farm," where the phrase "All animals are equal, but some animals are more equal than others" is used to satirize the idea of equality. In the context of polynomials, the statement likely means that all polynomials are considered equal, but some polynomials have additional special properties or qualities that make them "more equal" in a certain sense.

Overview of lectures

1. Organizational remarks, Motivation, Review on elliptic curves and Riemann-Roch for projective curves - 02/21/2023.
2. Intersection forms on smooth 4-manifolds, Rational & Elliptic K3 surfaces, Topological classification - 02/24/2023.
3. Elliptic fibrations via Lefschetz pencils, Mapping class groups with monodromy factorization, Fiber sum and Geography - 02/28/2023.
4. Elliptic fibrations via Weierstrass models, Fundamental line bundle, Discriminants, Stable reduction - 03/3/2023.
5. Reduction of elliptic curves, Kodaira’s classification of singular fibers, Néron model, Tate’s Algorithm - 03/7/2023.
6. Fine moduli (space) of elliptic curves, Universal family, Automorphisms - 03/10/2023.
7. More on algebraic stacks, Stable curves in genus 1, Moduli stacks of curves, Deligne-Mumford compactification - 03/14/2023.
8. Fibrations of algebraic curves, Hom stacks of curves, Minimal resolution - 03/17/2023.
9. PGL_2 stack quotient, Moduli stacks of Weierstrass fibrations over P^1 - 03/21/2023.
10. Crash course in Arithmetic Topology : Grothendieck motivic classes / Point counts over finite fields, Étale cohomology with Weil conjecture - 03/24/2023.
11. Stratification of the moduli, Moduli space of coprime polynomials and morphisms, Work of Segal, Farb and Wolfson - 03/28/2023.
12. Motives and Cohomology of Hom stacks and Enumerating elliptic curves over function fields - 03/31/2023.
13. Integral / Rational points on Mbar_{1,1} over global fields, Heights on Stacks, Tate's algorithm via twisted maps - 04/4/2023.
